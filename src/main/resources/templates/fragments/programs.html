Support Programs

We believe in giving back. Explore our community support initiatives below.

Elderly Support

Our elderly support program offers free delivery, priority customer service, and exclusive discounts for customers aged 65 and above. We also partner with local care homes to donate essential goods on a monthly basis.

Child Support

For every order placed, we donate 1% of proceeds to children's charities across Europe. Our child support program also provides discounted school supplies and educational materials to families in need through our NGO partners.

Patryk Support

A special program dedicated to supporting independent developers and small creators. We provide platform access, promotional visibility, and mentorship resources to help individuals grow their projects from the ground up.

Want to Get Involved?

Whether you're an individual or an organization, we welcome collaboration on new support initiatives.
