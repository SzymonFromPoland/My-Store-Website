Information

Everything you need to know about shopping with us.

Shipping Policy

We ship to all EU countries and selected international destinations. Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Orders placed before 2pm are processed the same day. Free shipping applies to all orders over $50.

Returns & Refunds

You may return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in original packaging. To start a return, contact our support team with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

Payment Methods

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Privacy & Data

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal data is never sold to third parties and is only used to process your orders and improve your shopping experience. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
